About Technoblade

Alex aka Technoblade 1999-2022

Technoblade was a straight up legend in the Minecraft scene. dude had cracked PvP skills, razor sharp comeback and a heart big enough to give back whenever he could

He pulled in over 10m subs and had the whole internet locked in with his dream SMP chaos and bedwars domination and the wild ass potato war saga

Even when cancer tried to come at him he stood tall and fought that shit like a final boss. Same fire & same grit. Inspired the hell outta millions just by being real and never losing that humor
